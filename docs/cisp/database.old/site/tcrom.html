Site Notes

CISP: A path leading to Trencrom fort. Trencrom fort lies approx. 0.5km to the SW. It is by no means certain that the stone would have been associated with this site, especially as it is uncertain it was ever inscribed.

No information for find-spot of stone.

Trencrom fort is a major neolithic and Iron Age fort, never excavated but which Thomas/1994, 194--195, suggests might have been an important site in the early middle ages.
