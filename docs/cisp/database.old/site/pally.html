Site Notes

RCAHMW/1925, 292: `The church is formed of chancel...nave...south transept...north transept...tower at west end and south porch. All parts are vaulted. There are no carved details throughout the church, and the arches are perfectly plain. The building is probably entirely of the same period -- the latter half of the 13th century. Both transepts are connected with the chancel by a diagonal passage across each angle. All the windows are modern'.
