Site Notes

The Parish Church of St. Cian was first mentioned in the 13th century and it is from this date that the earliest masonry in the nave probably derives. The remainder of the church dates to the 15th century and later. The church has a simple rectangular plan with a modern north vestry and west porch. The church has a noteworthy 15th century roof which has been much repaired (seeRCAHMW/1964, 57--58, and Plates 24, 26, 37 and 40).
