Site Notes

Okasha/1993, 97: On the basis of comments by H.L. Jones Okasha seems to indicate that the stone may only have arrived in its present position in the nineteenth century. If so any discussion of an `original' site becomes very difficult.

Okasha/1993, 97, discusses a fifteenth century church tower at the site.
