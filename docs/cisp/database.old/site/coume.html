Site Notes

Cuppage/etal/1986, 89: `This large headland, rising 328 feet/100m OD, projects W into the Blasket Sound at the extreme W tip of the Dingle Peninsula. A bank and external fosse, with a low outer bankm run N-S across the headland for about 500m...Macalister was informed that a souterrain, containing bones and a cross-inscribed stone, had once been uncovered on the headland'.
