Site Notes

RCAHMW/1925, 69: `The present building, with the exception of the tower, is modern, having been erected in 1853, in place of one which dated only from 1839. The latter had ``for reasons of safety to be levelled with the ground'' ...The western tower (17 feet square) is of three storeys, with corbel table and renewed battlements; it is a good example of the Pembrokeshire tower of the 13th century. The west door has been modernised.'
