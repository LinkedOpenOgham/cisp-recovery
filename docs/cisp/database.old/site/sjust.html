Site Notes

St Just Parish Church.

The site of the Boslow stone (BOSLW/1) is only two miles away to the SW over a low hill (Thomas/1994, 293).

Okasha/1993, 243, states that the Church underwent restoration in the early 19th century at which time the stone was found.

No other information.
