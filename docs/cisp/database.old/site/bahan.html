Site Notes

Macalister/1945, 92: `Five ogham inscriptions were discovered in a souterrain on this townland. The souterrain was a passage 52 ' long, in three sections, 18', 16' and 18' long respectively, on a slight zig-zag plan and with no expansion at either end'.
