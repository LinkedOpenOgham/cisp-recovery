Site Notes

Jones/1846a, 428: `On the south-eastern side of the road from Newborough to Llangaffo, and on the farm of Brondeg'.

Jones/1846a, 427 and Edwards/1986, 30, argue that the church of Newborough has a twelfth-century font, perhaps indicating an even older foundation.
