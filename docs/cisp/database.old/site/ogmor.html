Site Notes

Macalister/1949, 162: `The stone was found in the course of repair work at Ogmore Castle in the parish of Merthyr Mawr, where it had been built into a floor beneath the castle'.

RCAHMW/1976, 55: `It was discovered at Ogmore Castle in 1929 built into the base of a 19th-century limekiln'.
