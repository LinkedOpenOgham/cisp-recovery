Site Notes

RCAHMW/1937, 132: `the parish church of St. Michael stands in the western part of the parish. The earliest remaining details are of c. 1400, but the walls may be earlier'.

A 12th century church at Capel Lligwy is 1.3 miles away.

The site is not mentioned in any contemporary works. The church itself may date to before c. 1400, see RCAHMW/1937, 132.
