Site Notes

Westwood/1851, 145: `...the pretty little church of Baglan, near Neath, previous to its recent restoration, I found this stone lying exposed, face upwards, as one of the coping-stones of the churchyard wall. '

Rhys/1873, 7--8: `Baglan Church, about three miles from Briton Ferry station.'
