Site Notes

Jones/1847b, 42-43, states that the church of St. Cyngar was re-built in 1824, and the inscription formed part of the walls of the old church.

RCAHMW/1937, 90: `Parish church of St. Cyngar stands to the N. of the village and is a modern structure'.

RCAHMW/1937, 90, lists no features in the church older than the 17th century.
