Site Notes

RCAHMW/1925, 156: `This church has been deserted for nearly a century. It shows the ruins of a small building, comprising nave (18 feet by 8 1/2 feet), chancel (14 feet by 11 feet), and south door. The round-headed chancel arch still stands, but ruin is fast overcoming it. It is 8 feet high to the crown, 6 feet wide, and 2 1/2 inches thick. Round the nave there ran a low stone bench.

The ecclesiastical parish, with that of Llangolman, was united to Maenclochog by Order in Council of 11th July, 1877'.
