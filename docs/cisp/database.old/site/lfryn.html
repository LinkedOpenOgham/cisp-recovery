Site Notes

Westwood/1876, 69: `...church of Llanfrynach (Llan-vrynach, or the Church of St. Brynach, an Irishman who is said to have accompanied Brychan Brycheiniog into Wales in the fifth century). This parish lies about three miles to the south-east of Brecknock, and was a place of Roman occupation.'

Macalister/1949, 135: `...parish church...demolished and rebuilt in the year 1855.'
