Site Notes

See RCAHMS/1982, 245, 250--251 for a detailed discussion of this: `the principal burial-ground of Iona...Most of the funerary monuments...[recorded from Iona], both Early Christian and medieval in date, originally lay there'.

RCAHMS/1982, 245, 250--251, argues that this site was `the principal burial-ground of [the community at] Iona'.
