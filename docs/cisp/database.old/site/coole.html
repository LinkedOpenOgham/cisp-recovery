Site Notes

Macalister/1945, 224: `the old graveyard of Killeendreena'.

OSullivan et al/1996, 274: `The enclosed area which measures 47m N-S x 42m E-W, slopes gently towards S and contains the foundations of five huts, an ogham stone, a shrine and a holed stone. Numerous uninscribed grave-markers occur in the SE and S portions of the site'.
