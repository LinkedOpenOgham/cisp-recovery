Site Notes

O'Sullivan/Sheehan/1986, 244, call the castle MacCrehin's Castle, cf. Macalister/1945, 232. The castle, of which only the enclosing earthwork now survives, lies on the lower S. slopes of Bentee mountain. For a discussion of the site and further refs, see O'Sullivan/Sheehan/1996, 380--381, no. 1114.
