Site Notes

Formerly a portion of an ancient churchyard,

NMRS/NO56SE22: `The gardens to the west of the Cathedral, called `The College Yards' are said to be the site of a ce'li De' foundation of Malcolm II (1105-1134). On the erection of the episcopal see, c.1150, the prior and brethren of the Culdee community became the chapter of the cathedral'.

Roughly contemporary are the round tower (c.1000), a hogback grave-marker and the cross-slab fragment.
