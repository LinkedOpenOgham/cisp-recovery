Site Notes

Forsyth/1996, 402: `The precise find-spot of the stone is unknown, but it was explicitly stated to be a `distance of some miles from any known ruins' [Brash 365]. The remains of a possible monastery have been identified at `Chapel Knowe', Lunna (NGR HU 485 691), but, though it may provide a general context for the stone, there is nothing which links the two more directly (MacDonmald & Laing 1968: 127-8; RCAHMS 1948: 77-8 No. 1280: Small et al.1973: 9)'.
