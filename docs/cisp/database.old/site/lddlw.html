Site Notes

Macalister/1945, 491, notes that it was found `in the Liddel Water, between Newcastleton and Hawick'.

Macdonald/1935-36, 33, notes that it was found along `the Liddel water at Brox, a house which stands near the road, about a mile on the Hawick side of Newcastleton'. Macdonald also states that the site was `a few yards below the junction of the river with the Ralton Burn'.

The stone was a chance find in a river, and the site has no `history' as such.
