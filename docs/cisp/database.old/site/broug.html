Site Notes

Collingwood/Wright/1965, 264: `about a 1/2 mile east of Brougham on the north side of the road to Appleby, immediately opposite the Countess pillar'.

Brougham was the site of a Roman military fort called Brocavum in the Antonine Itinerary. A number of Roman-period inscriptions survive from this site, RIB/nos. 772-788. While a Christian community of some sort is implied by the likely Christian character of RIB/no. 787.
