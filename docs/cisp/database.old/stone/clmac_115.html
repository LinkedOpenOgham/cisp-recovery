Names

  • [Ma]elchiaran (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
    Petrie/1872, 67, argues that the commemorand is the Maelciaran Mac Cuinn-na-mBocht who is recorded as dying in the annals for 1076 and 1079.

    Lionard/1961, 167 partially accepts this identification, but suggests another candidate who is recorded as dying in 1101.
