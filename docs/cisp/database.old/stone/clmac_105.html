Language:Goidelic (rbook)
Ling. Notes:none
Paleo. Notes:The inscription is in half-uncial with an initial textual cross. The OR has a contraction mark over it, with an oval-shaped O (as is the 2nd example) and a majuscule R; the D has an ascender which bends to the left; the F is half-uncial and the E is closed.

Names

  • Fechtnach (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
    Petrie/1872, 41, identifies the commemorand as Fechtnach, Abbot of Fore in Westmeath, who, according to the Annals of the Four Masters, died in 776.
