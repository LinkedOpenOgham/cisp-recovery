Names

  • Llogiddeas (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
  • Veddellemetto (Language: Goidelic; Gender: female)
    McManus/1991, 75: `The name of the commemorand is undoubtedly an early form of Fedelmid/Feidlimid, an i-stem derivative of the female name Fedelm (Latinised and Anglicised Fidelma. The third E of VEDDELLEMETTOI, therefore, may be erroneous (as Fedelm, Fedelmid cannot come from *Vedelm-, which would have given *Feidelm, with lenited m) or, as Juergen Uhlich has suggested to me, we may have to do here with a svarabhakti vowel (see n 6. 28). The name is also attested in 206 TELEDMEV.. which is almost certainly inverted and to be read VEDELMET[TO])'.
  • Municcona (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
    McManus/1991, 75: `The sept name AVVI MUNICONNA (*Ui Muinchon?) is not recorded to my knowledge. One might consider the possibility that the N is an error for R (cp. Muirchi)'.
