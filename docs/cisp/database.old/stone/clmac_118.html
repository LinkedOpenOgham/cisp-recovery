Names

  • Th[ad]gan (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
    Lionard/1961, 163, 165, identified the commemorand as the same Thadgann recorded on CLMAC/182 as the father of one Dubcen. The son of this Dubcen, according to Lionard, was the Agda who is recorded as dying in the Annals of the Four Masters for AD 980. Lionard allows 30 years per generation and thereby ascribes a date of death for Dubcen of c. 950, and one of c. 920 for Thadgan.
