Names

  • Fergus (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
    Redknap/1976-77, 243: `the preposition do causes 'lenition' of a following consonant, which in the case of f, took the form of its total loss. Hence when an f is lenited it might quite often not be written at all, since it was not pronounced; and this is the case here'.

    OCorráin/Macguire/1991, 97: ``man-strength'...Fergus has been confused with another old name, Forggus, and after the Old Irish period they are both rendered Fergus. Fergus is an extremely common name in the early period'.
