Names

  • Melitv (Language: Latin; Gender: male)
    Hemp/1923, 319, editorial note: `The personal name spellings like it are Melitus, Mellitus, Meletus, Melitius, Meletius, and Melito. But one would have expected to find the name in the genitive (most frequently) or the nominative. Possibly the S for some reason left uncut. It seems to occur in Gallt Melyd, ``Melyd's Ascent'', the Welsh name for Meliden. Pre-Norman English place names are fairly numerous in Flintshire and Meliden may stand for Melid-don, as the suffixes -den (valley) and -don (hill) are often confused in English place-names'.
