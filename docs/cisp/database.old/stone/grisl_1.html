Names

  • Lonamni (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
    Ziegler/1994, 196, argues that that Lonamni is the genitive singular O-stem from *Lonagni, with the suffix being from the diminuitive suffix *-gno, and LONA meaning `blackbird'. She also argues that the name gave Lonan.
  • Bari (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
    Ziegler/1996, 135, argues that the name is a hypocristic from *barro meaning `head'.
