Location:on site
At west end of the south aisle of Lismore cathedral (as of summer 1996). The stone is now mounted on a iron bracket attached to the wall in such a way that it can be rotated to see both sides.
Form:Incomplete Information
Macalister/1949, 108: `The central fragment of a small free-standing wheel-cross: on both faces the ring is recessed below the face of the cross, and the openings between the ring and the angles are deepened but not perforated'.

Note the very small size of this stone.
