Paleo. Notes:The inscription is in half-uncial. The initial OR has a contraction mark over it; the O is spherical; the first R is minuscule and the 2nd majuscule; the D is closed-bowed; the 1st E is three-pronged and the 2nd E is closed; the 2nd R, the I, L, and M's each have a short stroke to the right from either the top or bottom of the first ascender.

Names

  • Maelmaire (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
    Petreie/1872, 68: `This name which signifies the servant of Mary, was borne by a celebrated man in Clonmacnois, in the early part of the twelfth century, whose death is thus recorded by the Four Masters:- `A.D. 1106, Maelmuire, son of Mac Cuinn-na-mBocht, was killed in the middle of the Daimhliag of Clonmacnois, by plunderers'.

    Lionard/1961, 168, accepts this identification.
