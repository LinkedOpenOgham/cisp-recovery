Site Notes

Forsyth/1996, 103: `Brandsbutt is situated by the old road north from Aberdeenshire into Moray (the modern A96) specifically on the route to the hill-fort of Dunnideer'. Forsyth also discusses the excavation of the site revealing `twelve or thirteen stone-holes comprising a circle 25m in diameter' of a `Bronze Age monument'.
