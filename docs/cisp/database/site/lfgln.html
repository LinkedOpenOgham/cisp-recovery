Site Notes

Anon/1877, 337: `A visit was made to the little disused church of Llanfaglan...Two early crosses were observed built into the porch, one of which is peculiar as having a ship incised with the mast cutting through the shaft of the cross [Nash-Williams/1950, 88, No. 90]'.
