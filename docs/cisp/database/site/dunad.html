Site Notes

Forsyth/1996, 227--228: `The fortress of Dunadd, Dun Ad, `fort of the river Add', [Watson 1926: 45]...is situated on an isolated rocky hilltop, 54 m OD, above the flood plain of the meandering Add, 6 km north-west of Lochgoilhead'.

See Lane/Campbell/2001 for a full recent treatment of the archaeology and early history of this important Dalriadic site.
