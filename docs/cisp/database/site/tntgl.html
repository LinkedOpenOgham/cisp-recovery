Site Notes

Langdon/1896, 366: `Tintagel, anciently called `Dundagell', is in the Deanery of Trigg Minor. It is situated on the north coast of Cornwall'.

Okasha/1993, 291, notes that the first recording of the stone was in a modern secondary location as a gate-post.
