Site Notes

Macalister/1949, 109: `a field (the site, it is said, of an old cemetery)'.

Okasha/Forsyth/2001, 353: `Nothing is now visible at the site, described by Power ... as a cillín, which lies on the south bank of the River Brickey about two miles from Dungarvan. A large limestone bullaun stone was found there'.
