Site Notes

ONeil/1932, 105: `...sea-wall at Barmouth ...in the sand twenty feet below high water mark at Latitude 52 52" (i.e. on a line with the `W' of `CAMBRIAN RAILWAY' on O.S. map XXXVI N.W.).'

CISP: This oft-repeated Latitude measurement is out by some distance, and would put the site near Harlech rather than just north of Barmouth harbour.
