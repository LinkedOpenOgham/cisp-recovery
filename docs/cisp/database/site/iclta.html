Site Notes

Macalister/1949, 88, `Inis Cealtra [sic.] lies in Scariff Bay, an inlet of Loch Derg on the Shannon.'

Macalister/1916, 93--174, Plates VII-XXVIII, gives a full description of the site and its monuments.
