Site Notes

Macalister/1945, 7--8, records that the stone had previously been a ``kneeling-stone' on a dwarf wall surrounding a well dedicated to St Mobhi', and that at some later date it was moved to its current site: 'upright on a low altar-like structure north of the well'.
