Site Notes

Hamlin/2001, 60: `Inishmacsaint was an early church in Lower Lough Erne associated with St Ninnid, traditionally an active missionary saint. On the island site is a graveyard with the ruins of a multi-period church, a plain stone cross and once-extensive earthworks. An early ecclesiastical bell was also associated with Inishmacsaint'.
