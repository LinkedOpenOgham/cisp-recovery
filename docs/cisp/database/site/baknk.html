Site Notes

Macalister/1907, 107--108, `...found in the construction of a drain connected with the ruins of a Medieval castle on the farm of Mrs Donovan, Ballyknock'.

Macalister/1945, 84: `Fifteen stones inscribed with Oghams were found on this townland, acting as lintels over a drain at the Castle Farm'.
