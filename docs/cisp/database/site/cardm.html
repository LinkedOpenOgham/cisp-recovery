Site Notes

Okasha/1993, 85, states that the Church of Cardinham has a 15th century chancel.

Okasha/1993, 85, states that restoration of the Church took place between 1872 and 1877.

Langdon/1896, 354, provides drawing of the east end of chancel with the cross before its restoration.
