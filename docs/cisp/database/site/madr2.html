Site Notes

Thomas/1994, 290: `The church of Madron, mother-church of the town of Penzance and contained within the remnants of a large curvilinear enclosure'.

Thomas/1994, 302, note 49: `this is the former (?pre-Norman) Landithy'.

Thomas/1994, 290, states that the church was restored during 1935--6.
