Site Notes

Westwood/1876, 54--55: `Vaenor, or Faenor (about 3 miles to the north-east of Merthyr Tydvil), within 100 yards of the twelfth milestone on the turnpike-road from Brecknock to Merthyr, as a gatepost in a wall on the right-hand side of the road, and about 50 yards on the other side of the river Llysevoc'.
