Site Notes

Macalister/1945, 227, gives no information as to the site.

OSullivan et al/1996, 276: `Burial Ground. The site is located in poor land on the W bank of the Cummeragh river, a short distance above its entry into Lough Currane. It consists of a heavily overgrown enclosure containing a cross-inscribed ogham stone and numerous uninscribed grave-markers'.
