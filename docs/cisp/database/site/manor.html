Site Notes

RCAHMS/1967, 176: `a cairn of stones, on the hill-side about 220 yds. WNW. of the walled enclosure on the left bank of the Newholm Hope Burn mentioned under No.379'. The cairn was demolished by about 1934, `but its position is probably marked by a circular setting of stonework which can be seen in the position just stated, and in which there lies a concrete replica of the stone'.
