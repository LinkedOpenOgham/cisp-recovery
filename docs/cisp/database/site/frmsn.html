Site Notes

Forsyth/1996, 261: `the former kirkyard of Formaston in the parish of Aboyne, Aberdeenshire. ... the old kirkyard is surrounded by a wall which is perhaps too rectilinear to reflect the line of an old monastic vallum. Under the turf can be seen the remains of what is presumably the medieval church, but no early remains have been discovered except for the ogham-inscribed fragment'.
