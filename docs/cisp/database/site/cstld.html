Site Notes

Okasha/1993, 91, notes the earliest location is recorded by Leland (c. 1540) as being about 1 mile from Castledore.

Radford/1951, 1-119, and Rahtz/1971, 49--54, discuss the site of Castle Dore, an Iron Age hillfort, possibly re-used in the early middle ages.
