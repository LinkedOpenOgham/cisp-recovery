Site Notes

Macalister/1945, 160, records that the stone was: `Found acting as a lintel over an outhouse', and thus its original location is unknown.

Cuppage/etal/1986, 44: `At the lowest and westernmost point of the ridge extending W from Flemingstown mountain, on level pastureland'.
