Site Notes

Stephney-Gulston/1908, 235--236, states that the site was the field of Cae Capel on Maes Nonni farm in the parish of Llanllwyni, near the river Teify: `The site of its discovery is a little -- say 300 yards -- off Sarn Helen, which runs from south-west to north-east through the parish, and is known to many of the older parishioners as being a little below the surface of the neighbouring fields to Cae Capel'.

Stephney-Gulston/1908, 235, suggests there may have been a religious establishment at this site.
