Site Notes

Lewis Morris' MSS notes, cited in Owen/1896, 138, `In the highway by Tyddyn Holand, between Bodafon and Rhiw Leding, in Creuddyn, near Conwy...near to a rock called Crai'r nodwydd dur...There are a vast many ruins about this place, and it seems there was a town here in ye times of ye ancient Britons.'

RCAHMW/1956, 115, `The site is named on O.S. sheet 5 N.W. ...but the `vast many ruins' are no longer visible.'
