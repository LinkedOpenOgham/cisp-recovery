Site Notes

Macalister/1945, 48: `Two stones, one of red granite, rectangular in horizontal section...the second of the local grit...standing at a distance of 3'9'' apart. Between them is a boulder of bluestone...Excavation showed that the first stone is sunk to a depth of 1'6'', the second 1'10'': and that the intervening space is filled with a packing of rounded stones of the average size of a man's fist. No trace of an internment discovered'.
