Site Notes

RCAHMW/1925, 379: `the farm of Llandrudian'.

Rhys/1898a, 60, speculates as to whether there was once a church on the site because he feels that the stone and the place-name are suggestive of this. He also queries if there are any other archaeological remains that might give the two inscribed stones some context.
