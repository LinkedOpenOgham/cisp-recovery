Site Notes

Macalister/1945, 257--258, the first stone was: `...built into the walls of St Declan's oratory...now standing in a niche in the choir of the Cathedral'. The second stone was built into the walls of the now ruined Ardmore Cathedral. The third stone was found in the cemetery.
