Site Notes

Thomas/1994, 307--308: `Welltown, Cardinham, belonged to farmland just east of Cardinham castle, the Norman seat of the descendants of William I's general Turold, conquerer of the south-west, where the lords of Dinan were the most powerful family in twelfth century Cornwall'.
