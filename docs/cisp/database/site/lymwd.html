Site Notes

In the fly-leaves of Lewis Morris' copy of Geoffrey of Monmouth's Gesta Regum Britanniae is the note that a stone was found `At Llan-y-Mawddwy, in Merionethshire, in a stone wall near the churchyard' (Owen/1896, 136).
