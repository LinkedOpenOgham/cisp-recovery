Site Notes

A burial near to the house of Low Curghie, precise location unknown.

Craig/1997, 619, gives the site grid reference as 2129 5376 (noting that this is further south than is shown on the map at Thomas/1968, 101). He also notes `a number of cist burials have been recorded S.W. of Kilstay cottage, 600m to the N. of Low Curghie (c.NX 125381). There is also a supposed church site in the field 150m S.S.W. of this cottage'.
