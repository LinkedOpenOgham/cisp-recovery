Site Notes

Macalister/1949, 120: `a hilltop enclosure known as Finner Rath'.

Lacy/etal/1983, 173--176: `A circular platform surrounded by a fosse and an outer bank. ... The platform known as 'Finner Rath', is largely natural being the top of a small hill. Modern field fences run through the site. It is situated in good land but close to sand dunes'. Also see Lacy/etal/1983, p. 175 for a plan of the site.
