Site Notes

Macalister/1945, 452--453: `this stone was discovered by Borlase, at which time he could say no more than it stood `about four miles East (properly N.E.) of Michel'. Since this time the hamlet of Indian Queen's has come into being'.

According to Macalister/1945, 453, the village came into being after Borlase's statement in 1754.
