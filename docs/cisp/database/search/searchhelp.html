Search Help

Enter words to be searched for in the Inscription Text box. The following wildcards may be used:
  • ? - match any single character
  • * - match a sequence of characters
  • [abc] - match one of the characters abc
Thus IAC?T will match IACET and IACIT.
?esu* will match JESU, IESU, IESUS (search strings may be entered in upper or lower case)

If more than one word is given to search, then only inscriptions which match ALL the search words will be found. The CISP codes for matching inscriptions will be displayed, together with the exact strings matched. Click on the inscription code to link to the relevant page.

The strings are matched from the EXPANSION field in the TRANSLAT table, with all special characters removed.
