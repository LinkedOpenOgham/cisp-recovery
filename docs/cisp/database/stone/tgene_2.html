CISP: The lettering is a very angular Insular half-uncial. The A's are in the 'OC' form and the R, I, N and D have wedge-shaped finials. The Rs are in an angular half-uncial form, the bows of the A's are square and the minuscule N has a flat top. The half-uncial T is also angular. The D has a vertical ascender and an open bow and the S is half-uncial. Between the A and D of the second line there is a small and raised curved minuscule N.
