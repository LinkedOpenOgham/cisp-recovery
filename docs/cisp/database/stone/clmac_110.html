Names

  • Guariu (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
    Petrie/1872, 55: `the name `Guare', of which Guariu is the dative singular...was borne by two priests who died at Clonmacnois within a short time of one another. In the Annals of the Four Masters we read `A.D. 944. Guaire, priest of Cluainmicnois, died'. While in the Chronicon Scottorum we read `A.D. 943. Guaire, son of Maelacan, priest of Cluain-[Muc-Nois] quievit''.

    Lionard/1961, 164, accepts that the commemorand was one of these two, and dates the inscription accordingly.
