Names

  • Vlcagni (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
    Okasha/1993, 191: `Jackson took VLCAGNI as a primitive Irish name. The name occurs on Lewannick II and also on a stone from Llanfihangel-ar-Arth, Wales'.

    Thomas/1994, 262: `an Irish-named settler'.

  • Severi (Language: Latin; Gender: male)
    Okasha/1993, 191: `SEVERI is a Latin name. The same name occurs on a stone from Newchurch, Wales, although the text on this stone has been re-cut. The name SEVERVS occurs on a Gaulish stone from Viviers and also occurred on a lost Gaulish stone from Treves'.

    Thomas/1994, 262: `a Roman named father'.
