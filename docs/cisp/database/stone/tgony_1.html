Names

  • Nonnita (Language: Brittonic; Gender: female)
    Thomas/1994, 284: `British feminine'.
  • Ercilivi (Language: Brittonic; Gender: male)
    Thomas/1994, 283--284, argues the name is masculine and that it `contains a word meaning `grey, speckled, etc' (Welsh erch and British *liuo, Old Cornish liu `colour')'.
  • Ricati (Language: Brittonic; Gender: male)
    Thomas/1994, 283--284, argues the name is masculine and that it goes back to *Rigo-catus (`kingly' and `battle')'.
  • Ercilingi (Language: Brittonic; Gender: male)
    Thomas/1994, 283--284, argues this name is masculine and brittonic.
