Names

  • Retagin (Language: Goidelic; Gender: male)
    McManus/1991, 72: `The elements in the name of the commemorand are clear but do not occur together elsewhere in the corpus. RETA- is very likely a form of the divine name RITTA-, found in 250 RITTAVVECAS, 211 RITTAVEVECC and iv. RITTECC... The element -GIN is attested elsewhere ... but never, in the Ogam inscriptions at least, following a divine name. To my knowledge the name is not attested later. Its Old Irish form would be nom. *Rethgan (cp. Éogan).'
