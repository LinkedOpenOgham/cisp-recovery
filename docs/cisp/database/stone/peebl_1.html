Names

  • Nicholas (Language: Greek; Gender: male)
    The cult of St Nicholas is otherwise unknown in Britain before the 11th century (RCAHMS/1967,177). A. A. M. Duncan suggests the inscription was in capitals and read NINIAVI (1981).

    Nicholas is, however, a Biblical name, and thus could have been the name of a more local saint.
